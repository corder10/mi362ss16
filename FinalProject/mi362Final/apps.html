The WWF and Apple

"Apple and WWF are working together to protect life on our planet."

 

My Experience with "WWF Together"

I recently got the WWF Together app under Apple's section titled "Apps for Earth." It's an engaging app with stories about 16 different species of animals and 1 section specifically for the Earth itself. By using a beatufiul interactive presentation the WWF's app showcases many gorgeous photos of endangered species from dolphins to jaguars to even pandas.

 

"For 50 years, WWF has been dedicated to protecting the future of nature."

 

Personally, I loved using the app to explore the environments the animals lived in. For example, when I picked "Snow Jaguar," there was an interactive 3d image that moved as you moved your phone around. Then a standing jump challenge to compare your standing jump (mine was 3ft) to a snow leopard's (30ft). Then I got my picture taken with some of these awesome animals that you can see below!
